Privacy Policy

Hatchi is built to feel calm, supportive, and simple. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: March 2026

Hatchi (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care and transparency.

Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address
  • Fertility and health-related data you choose to enter, such as cycles, medications, blood results, and appointments
  • App usage data to help us improve the app experience

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Hatchi app
  • Display your personal data within the app
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Send optional reminders and notifications you choose to enable

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase services provided by Google.

Data may be stored on servers located outside your country, including in the United States.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers, such as Firebase, required to operate the app
  • If required by law or legal process

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your data within the app
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To request deletion, please contact us at hello@hatchitracker.com.

Health Disclaimer
Hatchi is a tracking and support tool only and does not provide medical advice. You should always follow the guidance of your healthcare provider or fertility clinic regarding treatment, medications, and medical decisions.

Children’s Privacy

Hatchi is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
