Two Week Wait Tracker

A calm, supportive way to track the two week wait after embryo transfer.

What is the two week wait?

The two week wait is the time between embryo transfer and your pregnancy test. For many people, it can feel like one of the hardest parts of IVF.

Why tracking can help

During the two week wait, it is easy to lose track of medications, important dates, and how far past transfer you are. Having everything in one place can make the wait feel a little more manageable.

What Hatchi helps you track

  • 🐣 Days past transfer and key milestones
  • 💊 Medications and reminders
  • 📝 Symptoms and notes
  • 📅 Important test dates and appointments

Support during the wait

Hatchi is designed to feel calm, gentle, and supportive during a time that can feel emotionally heavy. It is a simple way to keep everything together without relying on spreadsheets.
