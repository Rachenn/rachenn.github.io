# Delete Your Account **Last updated:** March 2026 At Hatchi, you have full control over your data. ## How to Delete Your Account You can delete your account and all associated data by: 1. Opening the Hatchi app 2. Navigating to your profile/settings 3. Selecting "Delete Account" ## What Happens When You Delete Your Account When you delete your account: * Your account will be permanently removed * All associated data (including cycles, medications, blood results, and reminders) will be deleted ## Alternative Request If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting: [hello@hatchitracker.com](mailto:hello@hatchitracker.com) Please include the email associated with your account. ## Data Retention We do not retain your personal data after account deletion, except where required by law. ## Contact If you have any questions, please contact: [hello@hatchitracker.com](mailto:hello@hatchitracker.com)
