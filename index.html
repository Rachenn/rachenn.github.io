About Hatchi 🐣

I’m building Hatchi to make fertility treatment tracking feel simpler, calmer, and more supportive.

After going through fertility treatment myself, I found myself relying on a spreadsheet to keep track of blood results, medications, and appointments. I still could not find an app that did everything I wanted in one place, so I decided to create it.

Hatchi is being made with care for people going through IVF and fertility journeys, with a focus on feeling cute, friendly, and stress-free when things already feel overwhelming.

I’ll be looking for testers soon once it’s ready for the Google Play Store, so feel free to get in touch if you’d like to be part of it.
