About Hatchi 🐣

I’m building Hatchi to make fertility treatment tracking simpler and more supportive.

After going through many fertility treatments, I found myself living with a spreadsheet to track all my blood results, medications and appointments, I have not yet found an app that actually does what I want, so I thought, why not create it? While I'm making it for myself I feel others could truly benefit from Hatchi as well!

This app is being made with care for people going through IVF and fertility journeys, focusing on a cute, friendly, and stress-free experience.

I will be looking for testers soon once it's ready for the Google play store, so feel free to get in touch if you're interested.
