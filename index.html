About Hatchi 🐣

I’m building Hatchi to make fertility treatment tracking feel simpler, calmer, and more supportive.

After going through fertility treatment myself, I found myself relying on spreadsheets to track blood results, medications, appointments, and cycle details. I could not find an IVF tracker app that did everything I wanted in one place, so I decided to create it.

Hatchi is being made with care for people going through IVF and fertility journeys, with a focus on feeling calm, friendly, and supportive when things already feel overwhelming.

I’ll be looking for testers soon as Hatchi gets ready for Android, so feel free to get in touch if you’d like to be part of it.
